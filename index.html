My Co-op experience

I was given the opportunity to relocate to Montreal, Quebec to further my learning and work as a software development and machine learning engineer for a startup company called Healthcare Systems R&A Inc. The company itself has many different projects and utilizes many machine learning and artificial intelligence concepts to enhance small companies.

The project I worked on was called RDT Imaging. The problem is that rapid diagnostic tests (RDT) are used as a method to provide a rapid and effective way to diagnose certain diseases on the presence of certain antibodies, proteins and analyte concentrations of interest. However, many times RDT's result in faint lines and are therefore misinterpreted.

The solution was to create a computer assisted diagnosis software that can target the issue of misreading RDTs. Therefore, the aim of the project was to create an automatic reader that can read tests irrespective of the company and not be image specific or test specific and provides the concentration of the analyte tested. To achieve this, many different machine learning concepts and different image processing techniques were used to achieve more accurate results and have a decreased error rate.
